Unit 1: Seminar Preparation

LLM-Powered Planning Agent Project

Type: Preparation Activity | Status: ✓ Complete

Seminar Preparation Activities

Overview

Before attending the introductory seminar, I undertook comprehensive preparation to understand the module structure, assessment requirements, and project expectations. This preparation enabled me to make the most of the seminar and establish a clear foundation for the project work.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the overall structure and assessment requirements of the module
  • Identify key technical areas to develop throughout the module
  • Prepare targeted questions for the introductory seminar
  • Develop a clear understanding of teamwork and collaboration expectations

Preparation Activities

1. Module Structure and Assessment Review

I reviewed the Unit 1 seminar guidance to understand the three assessments: team design proposal, practical implementation, and individual reflection. Understanding how these assessments build upon each other gave me clarity on the overall module objectives and my responsibilities.

2. Group Work and Collaboration Expectations

I reviewed guidance on team collaboration and peer assessment. Key insights included understanding that team work is assessed both on the final product and the process, individual contributions are tracked separately, and clear communication is essential for demonstrating my contribution.

3. Skills Gap Analysis

I identified areas to strengthen during the module:

  • Designing LLM-powered planning agents
  • Selecting appropriate tools, APIs, and models
  • Creating system diagrams and technical documentation
  • Justifying technical decisions with academic literature
  • Building and demonstrating working prototypes

4. Questions for the Seminar

Project Scope: What planning tasks should the agent perform? What complexity level is expected?

Teamwork: How will teams be formed? How are individual contributions assessed?

Assessment: What distinguishes excellent work? How much weight is given to novelty versus feasibility?

Technology: Which LLM models and frameworks are recommended? Are there constraints on tools and APIs?

Reflection

This preparation was invaluable in establishing a solid foundation. By reviewing assessment requirements in advance, I arrived at the seminar with specific questions rather than vague expectations. The skills gap analysis helped me identify areas to develop proactively, and prepared questions ensured active, productive engagement with the seminar content.

This experience reinforces the value of thoughtful preparation. Understanding expectations, identifying gaps, and formulating questions significantly enhances engagement and learning outcomes.

Key Takeaways

  • Understanding assessment requirements in advance provides essential context for planning
  • Identifying skills gaps early enables targeted development
  • Prepared questions enhance engagement in collaborative sessions
  • Team collaboration requires clear understanding of individual and collective responsibilities
  • Thoughtful preparation significantly improves learning outcomes
